# Series Thumbnail Concepts

## Concept 1: Secret Glow In The Cradle
Baby Lio lies in the moon-carved cradle with one tiny hand hidden under the blanket, creating a small silver-blue glow. The nursery floor has faint Thread magic seams, and Mira's suspicious silhouette appears below/behind the floor as if the house itself is listening.

Strength: Immediately communicates the hook: infant protagonist, secret magic, hidden danger.

## Concept 2: Split Floor Eavesdrop
A vertical split composition showing the nursery above and magic lesson below. Lio listens from the cradle while Mira practices candle-glow underneath.

Strength: Very accurate to chapter 1, but slightly busier at thumbnail size.

## Concept 3: The Manor Heard Him Back
Veyr Manor at night with one nursery window glowing silver-blue and Thread diagrams faintly appearing in the walls.

Strength: Atmospheric and mysterious, but less character-forward.

## Selected Thumbnail
**Concept 1: Secret Glow In The Cradle.**

It best sells the series at a glance: a reborn baby hiding magic, the old house responding, and the first bond/threat with Mira.
