Reborn as the toddler son of a minor frontier noble house, Ren Vale hides his adult memories while secretly learning magic from nursery songs. But the lullabies that teach him Hearthsong Magic are also keys to the Ember Veil, an ancient sealed battlefield beneath his family's land. To protect his home, Ren must master forbidden magic without letting the Crown discover that a two-year-old can hear the old fires waking.
